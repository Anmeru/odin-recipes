Papaya Salad Recipes

Papaya Salad Also known as 'Som Tum', Thai Papaya Salad is a fan favourite in Thai cuisine, and super easy to make as a weekday meal.


Ingredients Needed

  • Shreded Strips Green Papaya
  • Chillies
  • Long Green Beane
  • Limee
  • Fish Sauce
  • Pungent Fermented Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Add chillies and long green beans
  2. Add limes, fish sauce, and pungent fermented fish sauce
  3. Mix all of them together
  4. Add the green papaya
  5. Mix again
  6. Done
